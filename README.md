# Idea Orbit

Idea Orbit is a powerful idea management platform designed to help users capture, organize, and prioritize their ideas effectively. With robust features including real-time notifications, markdown support, and location-based functionalities, users can manage their creative processes seamlessly.

## Features
- **Real-time Notifications**: Stay updated with instant alerts on your ideas and updates.
- **Markdown Support**: Easily format your thoughts and ideas for better readability.
- **Location Features**: Utilize location data to enhance your idea management experience.

This platform integrates with Firebase for secure data storage and management, ensuring your ideas are safe and accessible at all times.
